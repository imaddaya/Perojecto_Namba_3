How Does AI Impacts Society

Effects On Kids

Positively

Artificial Intelligence, or AI, is like a smart friend that helps us in many ways. It predicts the weather, helps us learn new things, and even assists doctors in hospitals. With AI, we can solve big problems, learn faster, and stay healthy. It's like having a super helper by our side!

Negatively

While Artificial Intelligence, or AI, can be helpful, it also has downsides, especially for kids. Some worry that too much screen time with AI devices might make it harder for kids to play outside or talk to real friends. It's important for kids to balance their time with AI and other activities for a healthy lifestyle.

Effects On Adults

Positively

Artificial Intelligence (AI) streamlines tasks, boosts productivity, and improves decision-making in businesses. It enhances healthcare with early disease detection and personalized treatments. AI innovations simplify daily routines, offering adults more leisure time.

Negatively

AI may lead to job displacement, perpetuate biases, and pose privacy risks. Overdependence on AI can diminish critical thinking skills and autonomy among adults. Addressing these concerns is crucial for a balanced integration of AI into society.

Effects On Elder People

Positively

Artificial Intelligence (AI) simplifies tasks, enhances productivity, and aids decision-making for businesses. It improves healthcare with early disease detection and tailored treatments. AI innovations streamline daily tasks, providing more leisure time for older individuals.

Negatively

AI may contribute to job displacement, perpetuate biases, and pose privacy risks for older individuals. Overreliance on AI can weaken critical thinking skills and independence among the elderly. Addressing these concerns is vital for a balanced integration of AI into society for older adults.
